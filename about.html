About AIMAILABS

AI Engineering Reasoning Lab

AIMAILABS Sdn Bhd is an AI engineering reasoning lab focused on the offshore energy sector. We build AI infrastructure that connects inspection, engineering assessment, and maintenance planning into a unified intelligence layer — spanning structural, mechanical, piping, electrical, instrumentation, civil, and safety disciplines.

Our flagship platform is INSPECTA — supported by COREVA for engineering reasoning and MAINTENA for maintenance execution. Together, they form a closed-loop integrity system that transforms inspection photographs into standards-based engineering decisions with full traceability.

The underlying AI architecture combines computer vision, knowledge graphs, graph neural networks, natural language processing, and multimodal AI. Every technology is purpose-built for safety-critical offshore environments, with fully offline operation and human-in-the-loop validation.

Based in Malaysia, serving Southeast Asia.

Our Infrastructure

Three Systems. One Lifecycle.

Inspect it. Assess it. Fix it. — Three purpose-built systems covering every stage of the offshore structural integrity lifecycle.

INSPECTA
"Find It" — AI Inspection Infrastructure for Offshore Structural Integrity
📱
INSPECTA Edge
AI In Your Inspector's Hands

Fully offline mobile app on an Ex-rated device. Photograph a defect — AI detects it, assesses severity against your standards, and generates a PDF report before you climb down.

☁️
INSPECTA Cloud
Real-Time Command Centre

Web dashboard where findings from every inspector and camera flow in real-time. KPI cards, severity breakdowns, and approval workflows — all in one screen.

📷
INSPECTA Vision
AI Eyes That Never Blink

Camera systems detecting structural defects in real-time — from portable campaign kits, to permanent installations, to autonomous robotic platforms.

🖥️
INSPECTA Core
The Engineering Desktop Hub

Desktop engineering workstation. Knowledge Graph assessment engine, inspection workspace, planning workspace, and SAP Work Order integration — built for the lead structural engineer.

COREVA
"Assess It" — AI Engineering Assessment & Predictive Analytics
📊
COREVA Reports
AI-Generated Analysis Reports

Structured engineering analysis reports generated from inspection findings. Standards-referenced, audit-ready, and traceable from defect photo to conclusion.

⚡
COREVA Anode
Anode Depletion Trending

Track cathodic protection anode consumption across your asset portfolio. AI-driven depletion curves, remaining life estimates, and replacement scheduling.

🔮
COREVA Predict
Predictive Integrity Analytics

Risk-based inspection planning powered by degradation trending and historical findings data. Predict where the next critical defect appears before it becomes a hazard.

🛡️
COREVA Fitness
Fitness-for-Service Assessment

When a defect exceeds inspection criteria, determine if you can still operate. AI-assisted FFS screening with full calculation traceability — accept, repair, or monitor, with documented engineering justification.

📏
COREVA Thickness
Wall Thickness Trending

UT wall thickness management across your CML network. Historical trending, short-term and long-term corrosion rates, remaining life calculations, and next inspection date scheduling.

🔁
COREVA Fatigue
Structural Fatigue Life Tracking

Cumulative fatigue damage tracking against S-N curves and joint classification per design codes. Remaining fatigue life estimates per member and joint — critical for aging jackets and topsides.

⚖️
COREVA Weight
Topsides Weight Management

Track cumulative weight changes and centre-of-gravity drift from structural modifications and equipment changes. Flag weight budget exceedances and link back to structural capacity assessments.

MAINTENA
"Fix It" — AI Maintenance Planning for Offshore Structural Repair
🔧
MAINTENA Scoper
From Finding to Work Scope

AI-powered work scope generator. Turns approved inspection findings into a consolidated, prioritised scope of work — complete with repair methods, resource plan, man-hours, and critical path sequence.

📅
MAINTENA Planner
Coming Soon

Maintenance scheduling and work order lifecycle management. From scope approval to execution tracking — integrated with SAP PM.

💰
MAINTENA Cost
Coming Soon

Cost estimation and budget planning for structural repair campaigns. Resource-based costing with contract-confidential unit price management.

📦
MAINTENA Packs
Digital Work Pack Generator

Auto-generates complete offshore work packs from approved scopes — method statements, risk assessments, material lists, quality plans, ITP, and permit requirements bundled as a single downloadable package.

📍
MAINTENA Track
Repair Execution Tracker

Real-time progress tracking of work scopes offshore. Which items are complete, in-progress, deferred, or rejected — with photo evidence linkage back to INSPECTA findings. Live campaign visibility for onshore teams.

✅
MAINTENA QA
Post-Repair Verification

Capture completion evidence — photos, NDT results, coating records — run against acceptance criteria, and close the loop back to the original finding. Full lifecycle traceability from defect to verified repair.

🔀
MAINTENA MoC
Management of Change

Manage the MoC process for structural modifications that deviate from like-for-like repair. Engineering review, weight impact, risk assessment, and approval workflow — linked to COREVA Weight and the original finding.

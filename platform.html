Platform Architecture

Three Systems. One Intelligence Backbone.

Each system targets a different team, a different workflow, and a different operational problem — all grounded in the same AI reasoning infrastructure.

Inspect
INSPECTA

Multi-discipline AI inspection infrastructure. Detects anomalies across structural, mechanical, piping, electrical, instrumentation, civil, and safety systems from inspection images and converts them into structured, standards-assessed findings. Operates across mobile devices, cloud dashboards, and camera systems.

Computer vision anomaly detection — multi-discipline image analysis — real-time severity assessment — offline-first architecture — audit-ready report generation

Assess
COREVA

Engineering reasoning engine. Evaluates how anomalies affect asset components across all disciplines using knowledge graph reasoning and graph neural networks aligned to international standards.

Integrity evaluation — degradation trending — fitness-for-service screening — predictive inspection planning — cross-discipline assessment logic

Maintain
MAINTENA

Maintenance planning and execution. Transforms engineering findings from any discipline into actionable repair work scopes, digital work packs, and execution tracking workflows — closing the loop from anomaly detection to verified repair.

AI-generated work scopes — digital work pack assembly — repair execution tracking — post-repair verification — management of change

AI

Shared intelligence backbone. All three systems operate on the same AI reasoning layer — computer vision, knowledge graphs, graph neural networks, NLP, and multimodal AI — ensuring that every finding, assessment, and repair action shares context and maintains full traceability.

Engineering Disciplines

Seven Disciplines. One Intelligence Layer.

AIMAILABS is not limited to a single engineering domain. The platform operates across all major offshore asset integrity disciplines — each with its own anomaly taxonomy, assessment standards, and maintenance logic, unified under a single AI reasoning architecture.

STR
Structural

Corrosion, fatigue cracking, member deformation, coating breakdown, weld defects, marine growth

MEC
Mechanical

Rotating equipment degradation, vibration anomalies, seal leaks, bearing wear, alignment drift

PIP
Piping

Wall thinning, flange leaks, support failures, CUI indicators, valve condition, dead-leg corrosion

ELE
Electrical

Cable tray degradation, junction box condition, earthing integrity, lighting system defects, heat damage

INS
Instrumentation

Instrument tubing condition, transmitter housing damage, impulse line integrity, panel corrosion

CIV
Civil

Concrete spalling, rebar exposure, foundation settlement, grout condition, drainage degradation

SAF
Safety Systems

Fire protection condition, safety signage, escape route integrity, deluge system status, passive fire proofing

Product Infrastructure

Inspect It. Assess It. Fix It.

Three purpose-built systems covering every stage of the offshore asset integrity lifecycle — across all engineering disciplines.

INSPECTA
Multi-Discipline AI Inspection Infrastructure — Detect and assess anomalies across all engineering disciplines at the point of inspection
EDG
INSPECTA Edge
Offline Mobile Inspection Intelligence

Fully offline mobile application deployed on Ex-rated devices. Inspectors photograph an anomaly — whether structural corrosion, a piping leak, an electrical deficiency, or instrument degradation — the system detects it, assesses severity against discipline-specific standards, and generates an audit-ready report before the inspector leaves the location. No connectivity required.

CLD
INSPECTA Cloud
Centralised Inspection Command

Web-based dashboard where findings from every inspector and camera system converge in real-time. Severity breakdowns, KPI tracking, and approval workflows — providing complete campaign visibility for onshore engineering teams.

VIS
INSPECTA Vision
Automated Visual Monitoring

Camera systems performing continuous anomaly detection across asset disciplines. Deployable as portable campaign kits, permanent installations, or autonomous robotic platforms — extending inspection coverage beyond human access.

COR
INSPECTA Core
Engineering Desktop Workstation

Desktop engineering environment integrating the knowledge graph assessment engine, inspection workspace, planning workspace, and enterprise system integration. Built for the lead integrity engineer's daily workflow across all disciplines.

COREVA
Engineering Reasoning Engine — Standards-aligned assessment and predictive integrity analytics
RPT
COREVA Reports
Structured Engineering Analysis

Engineering analysis reports generated from inspection findings across any discipline. Standards-referenced, fully traceable from the original anomaly photograph to the engineering conclusion. Audit-ready documentation produced in minutes, not weeks.

ANO
COREVA Anode
Cathodic Protection Trending

Track anode consumption across the asset portfolio. AI-driven depletion curves, remaining life estimates, and replacement scheduling — turning cathodic protection management into a data-driven operation.

PRD
COREVA Predict
Predictive Inspection Planning

Risk-based inspection planning powered by degradation trending and historical findings data across disciplines. Identifies where the next critical anomaly is likely to develop before it reaches an actionable threshold.

FFS
COREVA Fitness
Fitness-for-Service Screening

When an anomaly exceeds inspection criteria, determine whether continued operation is justified. AI-assisted FFS screening with full calculation traceability — accept, repair, or monitor, with documented engineering basis.

THK
COREVA Thickness
Wall Thickness Management

UT wall thickness management across the CML network. Historical trending, short-term and long-term corrosion rates, remaining life calculations, and next inspection date scheduling — systematically applied.

FTG
COREVA Fatigue
Fatigue Life Tracking

Cumulative fatigue damage tracking against S-N curves and joint classification per design codes. Remaining fatigue life estimates per member and joint — critical for ageing offshore structures, piping systems, and rotating equipment supports.

WGT
COREVA Weight
Topsides Weight Management

Track cumulative weight changes and centre-of-gravity drift from modifications, equipment additions, and discipline-specific changes. Flag weight budget exceedances and link back to capacity assessments across structural and mechanical systems.

MAINTENA
Maintenance Intelligence — From engineering findings to verified repair, with full traceability
SCP
MAINTENA Scoper
AI-Generated Work Scopes

Transforms approved inspection findings into consolidated, prioritised scopes of work — complete with repair methods, resource plans, man-hour estimates, and critical path sequencing. From finding to work scope without manual reprocessing.

PLN
MAINTENA Planner
Coming Soon

Maintenance scheduling and work order lifecycle management. From scope approval to execution tracking — integrated with enterprise maintenance management systems.

CST
MAINTENA Cost
Coming Soon

Cost estimation and budget planning for multi-discipline repair campaigns. Resource-based costing with contract-confidential unit price management.

PKS
MAINTENA Packs
Digital Work Pack Generator

Auto-generates complete offshore work packs from approved scopes — method statements, risk assessments, material lists, quality plans, and permit requirements bundled as a single downloadable package.

TRK
MAINTENA Track
Repair Execution Tracker

Real-time progress tracking of work scopes offshore. Which items are complete, in-progress, deferred, or rejected — with photo evidence linkage back to INSPECTA findings. Live campaign visibility for onshore teams.

QAV
MAINTENA QA
Post-Repair Verification

Capture completion evidence — photos, NDT results, coating records, test certificates — validate against acceptance criteria, and close the loop back to the original finding. Full lifecycle traceability from anomaly detection to verified repair across all disciplines.

MoC
MAINTENA MoC
Management of Change

Manage the MoC process for modifications that deviate from like-for-like repair across any discipline. Engineering review, weight impact, risk assessment, and approval workflow — linked to COREVA assessments and the original finding.
